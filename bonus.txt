Extra Credit Features:

- Night Mode

	Users can change nightmode and lightmode in run time, graphics window adapts itself with minimum number of draws.

- Player Turn and Notifications

	Both text and graphics windows shows whose turn and notifications when its needed.

- Smart Pointers and Vectors

	Throughout the project vectors and smart pointers have been used to deal with memory allocation.

- Bomb Block

	The Bomb Block appears as a special block when the player clears 3 rows, allowing them to clear 3 columns around where they drop the “bomb”.
